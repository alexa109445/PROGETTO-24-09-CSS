Focaccia with caramelized onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizier with sweet caramelized onions, cranberries and Brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter.

Featured in:

Fresh cranberry recipeces that go beyond souce.

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon instant yeast
  • salt
  • lukewarm water
  • oil

For the topping

  • yellow onions
  • balsamic vinegar
  • Brie
  • Frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought:

In a medium sized mixed bowl mixt together all the ingredients untilthe flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tought I sometimes knead....
